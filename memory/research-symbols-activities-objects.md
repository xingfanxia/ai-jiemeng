### 1.1 飞翔 (Fēixiáng) - Flying
- **Aliases**: 飞行, 腾空, 飞起来
- **Category**: 活动类 > 移动行为
- **Traditional Interpretation**:
  - 原文: "梦飞上天，主大吉。" —《梦林玄解》
  - 梦见飞翔高远，主升迁显达
  - 飞而坠落，主有忧患
- **Conditions**:
  - 飞得高且稳: 大吉，事业顺利
  - 飞得低或费力: 有阻碍，需努力
  - 飞翔时恐惧: 对未来不安
- **Psychological Meaning**: 渴望自由、超越现实限制；自我实现的愿望；逃避压力
- **Fortune Rating**: 吉 (75/100)
- **Lucky Numbers**: 1, 6, 9

## Summary

This research document contains **140 dream symbol entries** across four categories:

1. **Activity/Action Symbols**: 45 entries covering physical movements, daily behaviors, emotional expressions, and medical activities
2. **Object Symbols**: 45 entries covering weapons, valuables, clothing, furniture, natural elements, and daily items
3. **Building Symbols**: 25 entries covering residential, commercial, religious, and special structures
4. **Life Event Symbols**: 25 entries covering marriage, birth, death, career, health, and major life transitions

### Key Patterns Identified:

1. **反占 (Reverse Interpretation)**: Many seemingly negative symbols (death, crying, fighting) actually indicate positive outcomes in traditional Zhou Gong interpretation

2. **Condition-Based Interpretation**: Most symbols have multiple interpretations based on:
   - Dreamer's identity (gender, marital status, occupation)
   - Dream details (action, color, state)
   - Time context (season, time of day)

3. **Source Integration**: Interpretations draw from:
   - 《敦煌本梦书》- Dunhuang manuscripts
   - 《黄帝内经》- Yellow Emperor's Classic
   - 《梦林玄解》- Ming Dynasty dream encyclopedia
   - 《潜夫论》- Eastern Han treatise

4. **Psychological Layer**: Each symbol includes modern psychological interpretation aligned with Freudian/Jungian perspectives

### Data Format Compliance:

All entries follow the JSON schema outlined in the PRD documents:
- Structured with id, name, aliases, pinyin, category
- Conditions separated into distinct objects
- Fortune ratings quantified (0-100 scale)
- Lucky numbers provided for cultural relevance
