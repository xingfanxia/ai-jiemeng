# Traditional Chinese Dream Symbol Research
# 传统中国梦象符号研究报告

**Research Date**: 2026-01-11
**Sources**: 敦煌本梦书, 梦林玄解, 周公解梦通行本, 周公解梦原版七言诗

---

## Overview / 概述

This document compiles authentic traditional Chinese dream interpretations from classical sources including:
- **敦煌本梦书** (Dunhuang Dream Books) - Tang Dynasty manuscripts, highest academic value
- **梦林玄解** (Dream Forest Mysterious Interpretations) - Ming Dynasty 34-volume compilation
- **周公解梦** (Zhou Gong Dream Interpretation) - Popular version with 27 categories, 900+ seven-character poems

### 5.1 Five-Element Correspondence (五行配合)

### 5.2 Time-Based Interpretation (十二时辰)

Dreams at different times may have different meanings:
子时(23:00-01:00), 丑时(01:00-03:00), 寅时(03:00-05:00), 卯时(05:00-07:00), 辰时(07:00-09:00), 巳时(09:00-11:00), 午时(11:00-13:00), 未时(13:00-15:00), 申时(15:00-17:00), 酉时(17:00-19:00), 戌时(19:00-21:00), 亥时(21:00-23:00)

### 5.3 Dreamer Identity Factors (梦者身份)

Interpretation varies based on:
- **性别**: 男/女
- **婚姻状态**: 未婚/已婚/丧偶/离异
- **职业**: 商人/官员/农民/学生/士兵/僧侣
- **特殊状态**: 孕妇/病人/老人/儿童

### 5.4 Five Non-Interpretation Rules (五不占)

Do not interpret dreams when:
1. 神魂未定而梦者
2. 梦而未终中途惊醒者
3. 寤知凶厄者
4. 醒后忘佚大半者
5. 忘忽杂梦者
